NATO Membership Timeline

1949

Founding members join NATO: United States, Canada, United Kingdom, France, Italy, Belgium, Netherlands, Luxembourg, Norway, Denmark, Iceland, and Portugal.

1952

Greece and Turkey join NATO.

1955

West Germany becomes a NATO member.

1982

Spain joins NATO.

1999

Czech Republic, Hungary, and Poland join NATO.

2004

Bulgaria, Estonia, Latvia, Lithuania, Romania, Slovakia, and Slovenia join NATO.

2009

Albania and Croatia join NATO.

2017

Montenegro becomes a NATO member.

2020

North Macedonia joins NATO.

2023

Finland joins NATO.

Expected 2024+

Sweden has completed accession protocols and is expected to join soon.
